About Us

Welcome to UKBIT

SUKBIT is more than just a brand – it's a celebration of Filipino craftsmanship and sustainable fashion. We create handwoven bags that blend traditional techniques with modern design, bringing you accessories that are both beautiful and functional.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote the art of Filipino weaving while creating sustainable, high-quality bags that empower local artisans and delight customers worldwide.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: We use eco-friendly materials and traditional methods that have minimal environmental impact
  • Craftsmanship: Each bag is meticulously handwoven by skilled Filipino artisans
  • Quality: We never compromise on materials or construction
  • Community: We support local weaving communities and fair trade practices
  • Innovation: We blend traditional techniques with contemporary design

Why Choose SUKBIT?

When you choose SUKBIT, you're not just buying a bag – you're investing in a piece of Filipino heritage, supporting local artisans, and making a sustainable fashion choice. Each bag tells a story of tradition, skill, and passion.

Our bags are lightweight yet durable, functional yet stylish – perfect for the modern individual who values both form and function. Whether you're heading to work, traveling, or enjoying a casual day out, there's a SUKBIT bag that's perfect for your needs.
