Returns & Exchanges

Our Return Policy

We want you to love your SUKBIT bag! If for any reason you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help with our hassle-free return and exchange policy.

Request a Return

Return Policy Details

  • Return Period: 14 days from delivery date
  • Condition: Items must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging
  • Shipping: Customer pays for return shipping costs
  • Refunds: Processed within 5-7 business days after receipt
  • Exchanges: One-time exchange allowed within 14 days
  • Custom Orders: Final sale, no returns or exchanges
  • Sale Items: Final sale, no returns or exchanges

Return Process

1

Request

Submit your return request through our online form

2

Approval

We'll review and approve your request within 24 hours

3

Ship

Send the item back to us using provided instructions

4

Process

Receive your refund or exchange within 5-7 business days

Exchange Policy

We offer one-time exchanges for items of equal or lesser value. If you'd like to exchange for a higher-priced item, you'll need to pay the difference in price.

Exchanges must be requested within the 14-day return period and the item must meet the same condition requirements as returns.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or has a manufacturing defect, please contact us immediately. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you.

Please take photos of the damage or defect and include them in your return request form to help us process your claim faster.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our return policy or need assistance with a return, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team. We're here to ensure you have a positive experience with SUKBIT.
